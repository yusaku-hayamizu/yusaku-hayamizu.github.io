* [[Gnuplot:http://www.gnuplot.info]] [#cd70ecef]

Gnuplot is a portable command-line driven graphing utility for Linux, OS/2, MS Windows, OSX, VMS, and many other platforms.

*** Ubuntu [#cf272128]

*** macOS [#w4dd6b56]
